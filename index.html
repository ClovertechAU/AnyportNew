Our Products

Karver Systems

This leading French Marine brand is structured around four elements: Innovation, Performance, Design and Service and we are proud to promote and stock the range in Australia.The Karver Free-Flying Sail Furlers set the standard for performance, safety and ease-of-use. Karver Systems are the rope handling specialists with their range of Halyard locks, Jammers, Blocks and other products. Full range can be viewed on the Karver Systems website

Ino-Block

These incredibly reliable, high load / low weight blocks utilise Textile Axle Technology. They were used extensively by sailors in the last edition of the Vendee Globe Single-handed yacht race with exceptional success. Full range can be found on the Ino-Block website

Multi-Mover

We are the Victorian agent for the Multi-Mover tow tractors and electric power tugs. Produced in the Netherlands, the Multi-Mover allows ease of movement and accurately manoeuvre trailers, caravans and trolleys over a level surface. Due to its special tyres and robust zero emission engine, the Multi-Mover electric power tug may be deployed on both hard and soft surfaces.(We handle all Tasmanian and South Australian enquiries) Full range can be found on the Multi Mover website

Marinaquip Docking Wheels

Marinaquip Docking Wheel - A rolling "dock bumper" to assist with safe and smooth docking manoeuvres. Docking wheels provide strength and cushioning and are designed to offer practical assistance with docking and manoeuvring in tricky conditions whilst simultaneously helping to protect both dock and boat from damage. Full product range on the Marinaquip website

Synergy PVC

Any Port Enterprises has a partnership with Synergy Textiles to supply fabrics in a huge range of finishes, styles and colours. Our fabric range includes materials with the following finishes: Waterproof, Breathable Waterproof, Hi-Visibility, Fire Retardant, Mildew Resistant, Anti-Bacterial, UV Stabilized, Reflective as well as Cut and Abrasion Resistant. Many of our materials are dye-sublimation printable and weldable. More information is available at the Synergy Textiles website

Fidlock

Produced by an innovative German company, Fidlock manufacture a range of magnetic fasteners that are simple to use, safer and a great design feature in any bag, cover or product that requires adjustable closing and fastening.
